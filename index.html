Insert your name and press ’Start Game’. Choose which level you would like to play in. You will then insert a number between 1 and 20. The bots will try to guess the number aswell. If your number is too low, you will be told to guess for a higher number and if your number is too high, you will be told to guess for a lower number. You will have 5 complete rounds to try and guess what number the computer has generated and the result will be shown in points.
